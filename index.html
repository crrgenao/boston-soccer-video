FAQ

Good things to know before kickoff.

How fast will we receive the video?

Most videos are delivered within 24-48 hours after the game.

Can we download the full game?

Yes. Each recording includes a private viewing link and a downloadable full-game file.

Can you livestream the game?

Yes. Livestreaming is available, but quality depends on the field connection and cell signal.

What happens if the game is cancelled?

If a game is cancelled due to weather, field conditions, or schedule changes, the booking can be rescheduled at no additional cost.

Do you require payment upfront?

No upfront payment is required during the launch stage. Payment is collected at the start of the game before recording begins.

Do you record youth games?

Yes. Youth games can be recorded when the team, parent, coach, league, or organizer has permission for recording.
